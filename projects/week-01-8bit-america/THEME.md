# Week 1 Theme: 8-Bit America

**Deadline:** Sunday 11:59 PM CT  
**Prize:** American-inspired BBQ box (1st place)

---

## The Concept

Build one weird little playable piece of America's 250th anniversary using **NES-era aesthetics** — pixel art, 8-bit interfaces, old-school interactions.

> "Small enough to finish and strange enough to remember."

No beige dashboards. No overly serious civic platforms. Something small, weird, and shareable.

---

## Build Ideas (pick one or invent your own)

- Pixel-art memory map — users pin American memories to locations
- NES-styled civic quiz game show
- Declaration of Independence as a side-scrolling adventure
- Branching "Choose Your Own Republic" game with civic tradeoffs
- Museum kiosk menu interface for America 250
- Fictional arcade cabinet mockup

---

## AI Rule

Use AI **like a power-up** — to enhance weirdness and execution, not replace it. AI can classify memories, generate content, or assist, but shouldn't sanitize the project's character.
